OUR STORY

PixelForge was born in 2015 from a shared love of classic arcade games and the golden age of pixel art. What started as a small indie studio in a garage has grown into a team of passionate developers creating games that blend retro aesthetics with modern gameplay.

Our mission is simple: create games that feel like they were pulled straight from your childhood memories, but with the polish and quality that today's gamers expect.

We believe in small, focused teams where every member has a voice in the creative process. This approach allows us to stay agile and innovative while maintaining the personal touch that defines PixelForge games.
