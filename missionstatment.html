OUR MISSION

Leveling up gaming experiences one pixel at a time

WHY WE EXIST

At Jupiter LightHouse, we believe games should be fun first. Our mission is to create memorable gaming experiences that blend retro charm with modern gameplay.

PASSION FOR PIXELS

We're obsessed with pixel art and the nostalgia it evokes. Every sprite is crafted with care.

GAMEPLAY FIRST

Flashy graphics mean nothing without solid mechanics. We prioritize fun above all else.

COMMUNITY FOCUSED

Our players shape our games. We listen, adapt, and create experiences you'll love.

CORE VALUES

1

CREATIVITY OVER CONFORMITY

We take risks and innovate rather than follow trends. Our games stand out because we dare to be different.

2

QUALITY IN EVERY PIXEL

From code to artwork to sound design, we sweat the small stuff so you don't have to.

3

PLAYER-CENTERED DESIGN

We design for players first. Every mechanic, every level, every pixel serves the experience.

HONESTY & TRANSPARENCY

OUR JOURNEY

2015

Founded in a college dorm by three friends passionate about retro games

2017

Released our first game "Pixel Jumper" which gained a cult following

2019

Expanded to a team of 10 and moved into our first office

2021

"Chrono Pixels" won Best Indie Game at the Pixel Awards

2023

Over 5 million players across all our games and growing
